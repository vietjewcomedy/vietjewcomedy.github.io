Joseph Nguyen

Stand-up comedian. Writer. Legitimate businessman.

Subtle but sharp, Joseph Nguyen is a smart slacker known for his easygoing delivery and solid writing. At home in all venues from clubs to cafes, Joseph is a regular at the Punch Line and other Bay Area clubs and has performed at festivals such as SF Sketchfest, Bridgetown Comedy Festival, and Tower City Comedy Festival.
